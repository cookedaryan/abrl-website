About Advanced Batteries Research Laboratory

Advanced Batteries Research Laboratory (ABRL) is located at Department of Energy Science and Engineering , Block 5, Indian Institute of Technology Delhi (IIT Delhi) . ABRL is dedicated to create a resilient energy future while mitigating environmental pollution, which is one of the most crucial challenges facing humanity today. At ABRL, we study new materials to address crucial challenges in energy storage landscape, spanning form high energy density advanced batteries to pseudocapacitors. We also work on stimuli responsive smart materials to monitor environmental degradation.
